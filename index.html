About the Author

A.J. Beckett writes romance centered on the high-stakes friction of human interaction. Her work explores longing, trust, vulnerability, and the emotional tension that builds when two people can no longer ignore what is pulling them together.

These are stories about intimacy, restraint, chemistry, and the complexity of choosing another person when it would be easier not to. A.J. Beckett lives and writes in Northern Alabama.
