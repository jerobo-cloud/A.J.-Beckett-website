About the Author

A.J. Beckett writes romance centered on the tension and connection between two people who cannot ignore what draws them together.

Her stories explore intimacy, restraint, vulnerability, and the emotional gravity that builds when desire finally breaks through hesitation.

A.J. Beckett lives and writes in Northern Alabama.
