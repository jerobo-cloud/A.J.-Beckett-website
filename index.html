About the Author

A.J. Beckett writes romance centered on the high-stakes friction of human interaction. It is a study of the structures we build together—and the desire that threatens to burn them down.

Beckett’s work explores the specific mechanics of intimacy: the heavy silence, the involuntary reach, and the electric heat generated when two people finally stop fighting their own dynamics. These are stories about the magnetic pull of another person and the profound complexity of choosing to stay.

A.J. Beckett lives and writes in Northern Alabama.
