About the Author

Romance grounded in emotional friction, vulnerability, and the charged space between resistance and surrender.

A.J. Beckett

A.J. Beckett writes romance centered on the tension and intimacy that grow when two people are drawn together before they are ready to admit it.

Her work explores chemistry, restraint, emotional risk, and the kinds of connection that quietly reshape a life. She lives and writes in Northern Alabama.

“These are stories about longing, vulnerability, and the choice to stay when walking away would be easier.”
