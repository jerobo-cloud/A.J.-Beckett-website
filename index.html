The Heat Between Us.

A.J. Beckett writes romance centered on the high-stakes friction of human interaction. It is a study of the structures we build together—and the desire that threatens to burn them down.

Exploring the mechanics of intimacy: the heavy silence, the involuntary reach, and the electric heat generated when two people finally stop fighting their own dynamics.
